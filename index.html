Black beans with tortilla chips

  • Time: 1hr 45minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Organic black beans 1 can
Whole kernel corn 1 can
Tortillachips 1 bag
Cilantro 1/4 cup
Virgin olive oil 1 tsp
Lemon juice 1 tsp

Step 1:Pour the black beans and whole kernel corn into a small bowl and drain and rinse.

Step 2:Mix all additional ingredients together in a large bowl(except the chips).

Step 3:Serve alongside the chips.

Cajun deviled eggs

  • Time: 35minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Hard boiled eggs cooked and de-shelled 4 to 6
Mayo 1 to 2 tbs
Mustard 1 tsp
Dill relish 1 tsp
Salt and pepper to your liking
Paprika 1 tsp

Step 1:Cook your eggs for 10minutes and then remove the shells.

Step 2:Cut in half and scoop out the yolks putting them in a seperate bowl.

Step 3:Add the rest of the ingridients except for the paprika.

Step 4:Stir until well mixed.

Step 5:Scoop the mixture and put into egg shells and top with paprika.

Cucumber salad

  • Time: 20minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Avocado - cubed 3
Cucumbers - cubed 3 to 4
Fresh tomatoes 1 cup
Virgin olive oil 1/4 cup
Mozarella Cheese - cubed 1 cup

Step 1:Mix all the vegetables and mozarella into a large serving bowl.

Step 2:Drizzle the virgin olive oil over the salad some minutes before serving.
